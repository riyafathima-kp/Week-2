About Brocelle Ice Creams

Since our beginning, Brocelle Ice Creams has been dedicated to crafting premium frozen desserts that bring happinessn to every customer. What started as exceptional desserts has grown into mordern creamery rooted in culinary integrity. We believe that icecream is more than just a sweet treat- it is a timeless vehicle for joy, nostalgia and celebration.That is why we refure to take shortcuts, ensuring that every pint,scoop and cone we serve delivers an unforgettable sensory experience.

Our Journey

2018
Brocelle Ice Creams was found in Kerala with a vision to create premium handcrafted ice creams
2020
Opened our first flagship icecream parlour at Kochi,Kerala
2021
Expanded to five outlets across Kerala and introduced home delivery services
2023
Reached twenty outlets and launched seasonal signature flavours inspired by local ingredients.
2025
Become one of the keralas most loved ice cream brands with over fifty outlets and thousands of happy customers.

Our Mission

"To transform ordinary moments into extraordinary memories through the art of premium ice cream. We strive to be the highlight of your day by consistently delivering rich, velvet textures and imaginative flavor combinations that challenge and delight your palate. By pairing culinary excellence with prompt, temperature-perfect delivery, we ensure that premium artisanal indulgence is always accessible, convenient, and deeply rewarding."

Our Core Values

Uncompromising Quality

We believe that exceptional ice cream starts with exceptional standards. From sourcing real, farm-fresh dairy to enforcing a strict sub-zero cold chain for deliveries, we never cut corners or use artificial fillers. Every scoop is guaranteed to be rich, smooth, and perfect.

Fearless Creativity

We treat ice cream as an open canvas for culinary imagination. Our team is constantly experimenting in the test kitchen, fusing classic techniques with bold, unexpected ingredients to invent unique flavors that surprise and delight your palate.

Customer Happiness

Your joy is our ultimate metric of success. Whether we are scooping at a live event, delivering a late-night pint to your doorstep, or handling a custom order, we pour passion into our service to ensure every interaction leaves you smiling.
